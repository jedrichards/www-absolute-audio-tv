I'm an experienced and reliable San Francisco based location sound recordist and mixer. I'm British and have recently relocated to San Francisco after freelancing in London for the last nine years. I'll be working predominantly out of the US and San Francisco for the foreseeable future, but I'm also available for UK jobs of 6 shoot days or more. I have a five year US L2 Visa, EAD work authorization and social security number.

I've worked on a variety of productions around the world for all the major broadcasters, including the BBC, MTV, Nat Geo and Discovery. I've very much enjoyed the experience so far and feel privileged to have a job that has introduced me to many fascinating people and allowed me to travel to wonderful places. I don't take myself too seriously, I'm enthusiastic and always willing to help.

I take pride in keeping my kit well maintained and up-to-date, and make sure to carry enough to immediately cover the needs of most jobs. See below for a comprehensive kit list.

Please take a look at my credits/CV/resume to see what I've been up to, and don't hesitate to contact me to discuss your upcoming projects.
