I'm Seb Blach, an experienced and reliable London based location sound recordist. During the last 9 years I have worked on a wide variety of programmes for all the major broadcasters, along side corporate shoots and commercials.

I've very much enjoyed the experience so far and feel privileged to have a job that has introduced me to so many wonderful people, and places. I don't take myself too seriously, I'm enthusiastic and always willing to help my team mates.

I take pride in keeping my kit well maintained and up-to-date, and make sure to carry enough to immediately cover the needs of most jobs. See below for a comprehensive kit list.

Please take a look at my credits/CV/resume to see what I've been up to, and don't hesitate to contact me to discuss your upcoming projects.
